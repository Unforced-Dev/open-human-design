# Soft-launch note for friends

A short, copy-pasteable message to send HD-interested friends. Edit the voice to
sound like you; the goal is honest feedback, not praise.

---

**Hey — I built a Human Design app and I'd love your honest take before I share it wider.**

It's **openhumandesign.com** — free, no account, nothing tracked, your birth data
stays on your device. The idea is to give away all the depth other HD apps paywall
(Variable/PHS, line-level detail, connections, transits, team charts) with calm,
accurate, modern design.

**Could you try a few things and tell me what you think?**

1. **Pull up your chart** — enter your birth info (it finds your timezone for you).
   Does it feel accurate? Does your type / authority / profile resonate?
2. **Tap around the bodygraph** — click a gate to read your *specific* line
   interpretations (e.g. "Gate 34, Line 5"). That line-level depth is the thing most
   apps charge for — is it useful? Does the writing land?
3. **Compare with someone** — use the Connection view, or hit **"Invite to compare"**
   on your chart and send me the link.
4. **Save your chart as an image** (the "Save image" button) — would you post that?
5. **If you use Claude or ChatGPT:** you can connect it so your AI pulls up your chart
   on demand. Ping me and I'll show you — it's kind of magic.

**What I most want to know:**

- Does the chart feel *right* — does it resonate?
- Anything confusing, missing, or clunky?
- Would you actually use this instead of whatever HD app/site you use now?
- How's the writing — does the interpretation land or feel generic?

Please don't be polite — "this annoyed me" and "I didn't get this" are the most
useful things you can tell me. 🙏

---

## What to listen for (for you, not them)

- **Accuracy/resonance** — if multiple people say their chart feels off, check birth-time
  handling first (the usual culprit).
- **First-30-seconds confusion** — where do they get stuck on the entry form or the chart?
- **Does the line-level depth land?** — it's the headline differentiator; if people skip it,
  the surfacing needs work.
- **Do they try the AI connector unprompted?** — if not, the "connect your AI" path needs to
  be more visible/guided.
- **Do they share anything?** — the Invite-to-compare link or the saved image are the viral
  loops; watch whether they actually use them.
